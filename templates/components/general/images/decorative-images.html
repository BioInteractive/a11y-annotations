Decorative Images

A decorative image is an image that is used purely for aesthetic or decorative purposes and does not convey meaningful content or information. Decorative images are typically added to enhance the visual design of a web page but do not serve a functional role in conveying information to users. A few examples include:

  • Ornamental borders used to create decorative borders around content or sections of a webpage.
  • Background images used in the background of a webpage to create a specific ambiance or atmosphere.
  • Background textures to enhance the visual appearance of a webpage.
  • Icons or graphics used purely for visual embellishment and not representing actionable elements.

  1. Identify all the images.
  2. Decide which images are purely decorative.
  3. Add a sticky note annotation to define the image as decorative.
