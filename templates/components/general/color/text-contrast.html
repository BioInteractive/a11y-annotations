Text Color Contrast

Standards

Regular sized text and images of text (text that is less than 18pt or 24px) must have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 against the background.

Large sized text and images of text (text that is more than 18pt or 24px or text that is bold and at least 14pt or 18.5px) must have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 against the background.

How to Test

Download and use TPGi's free colour contrast checker to measure the color contrast ratio of the text and the adjacent background colors.
