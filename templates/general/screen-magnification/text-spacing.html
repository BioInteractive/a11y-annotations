Text Spacing

No loss of content or functionality occurs by setting all of the following and by changing no other style property:

  • Line height (line spacing) to at least 1.5 times the font size;
  • Spacing following paragraphs to at least 2 times the font size;
  • Letter spacing (tracking) to at least 0.12 times the font size;
  • Word spacing to at least 0.16 times the font size.

  1. Adjust text spacing according to the specified criteria and ensure there is no loss of content or functionality.
  2. Inspect elements with text to verify that the increased spacing does not cause content to overlap or become unreadable.
  3. Ensure that all interactive elements are still functional and accessible with increased text spacing.

  • Text spacing adjustments cause content to overlap or become unreadable.
  • Warning: Ensure that text spacing can be adjusted without causing content loss or functionality issues.
  • Recommendation: Use CSS techniques to manage text spacing while maintaining readability and functionality.
