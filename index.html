Accessibility Annotation Repository

The Accessibility Annotation repository empowers design auditors to collaborate with Figma designers and streamline the preparation of designs for developer handoff. Designers receive guidance from auditors, who identify potential accessibility issues and provide recommendations based on accessibility requirements, specifications, and best practices. Auditors ensure that designs adhere to accessibility specifications, WCAG requiremenets and consistently exhibit expected behaviors, facilitating a seamless transition for developers. Additionally, the repository offers valuable tips and guidelines, encouraging designers to consider accessibility from the early stages of the product lifecycle.
